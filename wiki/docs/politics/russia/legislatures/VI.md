# 6th Supreme Soviet (since 4 June 2025)

The **6th Supreme Soviet** was elected on **4 June 2025** and comprises **100 seats**.
It is characterised by a **governing coalition** between the **RSDRP** and **Yabloko**, combining socialist and liberal forces in a pragmatic alliance.
The **KPRF**, **Rodina**, and **LDPR** form the **opposition**, representing the remaining left-wing and conservative blocs within the chamber.
