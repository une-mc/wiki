# 5th Supreme Soviet (January – June 2025)

The **5th Supreme Soviet** was elected on the **8th January 2025** and functioned until the **4th June 2025** election that brought in the 6th Legislature.  
It consisted of **100 seats**, divided between a left-wing **coalition government** and a strong **nationalist opposition**.

The **Communist Party of the Russian Federation (KPRF)** and the **Russian Social Democratic Labour Party (RSDRP)** jointly formed the **government**, controlling 60 seats. Their alliance promoted socialist economic planning, industrial reconstruction, and expansion of state welfare.  

The **Liberal Democratic Party of Russia (LDPR)**, holding 40 seats, served as the sole **opposition**, adopting a populist and nationalist stance against government policies.  

The 5th Legislature was marked by intense ideological debate and rising political polarisation, setting the stage for the June 2025 elections and the emergence of the RSDRP–Yabloko coalition that succeeded it.
